About the Hackathon

We are thrilled to invite you to Hack the Future 2025: Innovating Intelligent Data Insights, a national-level hackathon organized by IIEC, IIT Gandhinagar, in collaboration with the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI), Government of India.

This 36-hour immersive challenge will bring together developers, designers, innovators, and students to leverage AI and data science in addressing critical socio-economic and development challenges using publicly available data.
