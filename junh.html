PENTATHON Hackathon 2025

National Level CTF Challenge & VAPT Exercise for Cybersecurity Talent

About PENTATHON 2025

We are pleased to announce the launch of PENTATHON 2025, a premier national-level hackathon on Capture the Flag (CTF) Challenge and VAPT exercise, jointly organized by NCIIPC, AICTE, and MIC. This initiative aims to identify and nurture top talent in cybersecurity, and Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT).

This hackathon will provide participants with an opportunity to engage in real-world cybersecurity challenges, test their skills against the best minds in the country, and contribute to national cyber resilience.

Registration Deadline

The deadline for submitting your interest is June 15, 2025

Competition Stages

Stage 1: Online Qualification

A jeopardy-style Capture The Flag (CTF) challenge covering Web Attacks, Network Exploitation, API Vulnerabilities, OT SCADA, and more.

Stage 2: Physical Grand Finale

A high-stakes live cybersecurity exercise focused on protecting Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) from advanced cyber threats.

Stage 3: Bootcamp & Training

Comprehensive training and bootcamp for shortlisted teams to enhance their cybersecurity skills.

Eligibility Criteria

Student Eligibility

Regular Students (UG/PG/PhD) from Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) are eligible to apply for this hackathon.

Restrictions

No Professionals or startups can take part in this hackathon.
